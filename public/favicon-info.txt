# Favicon Setup

## Current Setup
- icon.svg: SVG icon (scalable, best quality)
- favicon.ico: Will be auto-generated by Next.js from icon.svg

## To Generate favicon.ico manually:
1. Use online tool: https://favicon.io/favicon-converter/
2. Upload icon.svg
3. Download favicon.ico
4. Place in /public/favicon.ico

## Or use ImageMagick:
convert icon.svg -define icon:auto-resize=16,32,48,64,256 favicon.ico
